What happens if users choose “Cancel”?
How to customize the options to users, e.g. only two options: “Yes” and “No”, OR “I do” and “I don’t” (Suggestion: Use Javadocs or using Eclipse/Netbean IDE help).

Answers:
 1. If users choose "Cancel", the dialog will return a message: "You have chosen: No"
 2. To customize the options to users, e.g. only two options: “Yes” and “No”, OR “I do” and “I don’t”, we can use JOptionPane.showOptionDialog with custom button labels.
